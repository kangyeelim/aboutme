"Try to see things differently - It's the only way to get a clearer perspective on the world and on your life"

IlluminateSG is a project under Social Impact Catalyst NUS which is a club that focuses on social entrepreneurship. Our aim as IlluminateSG is to spark meaningful conversations on pressing social issues amongst youths in Singapore. Currently, we have organised 2 of our flagship events called 'Common Space' where we bring together different people and guest experts to share on certain topics. The topics of the 2 'Common Space' events were 'Education Inequality','Mental Health' and 'Migrant Workers'.

We are still exploring how we can ensure that our events are sustainable through a revenue stream such that our project can work more like a social enterprise and further our cause. We are currently under a grant by the National Youth Council under the Young Change Makers program.

I am part of the Marketing team where I am the head of content creation . I overlook specifically at the content we create for our social media sites, our website where we share blogs and our marketing material for our events like posters and banners. I also do designs for our Instagram posts. I am incredibley delighted to be working with amazing group mates, especially the marketing team that is filled with talented individuals. Although we have our positions, we work on a flat hierarchy so that everyone is able to comfortably voice out opinions and be part of decision making. My marketing team mates include Raihana, a year 4 business marketing student in NUS who is also our head of public relations and marketing, Samihah, a year 3 political science student in NUS who is a writer of our website blogs, Tze Min, a year 3 business analytics student who is another writer for our website blogs Wei Ming, a year 2 computer science student in NUS who is in charge of our website and graphic design as well as Darima, a year 3 arts student in NUS who is a designer for our social media sites.
